# Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchase behavior in a retail fashion business using Microsoft Excel. The objective is to transform raw customer transaction data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, KPI calculations, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and an interactive dashboard.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer purchasing patterns, identify high-performing product categories, evaluate customer loyalty, and make data-driven decisions related to marketing, inventory planning, and sales optimization.

---

## Business Objectives

The project aims to answer the following business questions:

### Customer Segmentation

* Which customer age groups contribute the most revenue?
* How does purchasing behavior differ by gender?

### Product Performance

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which categories contribute the least to overall sales?

### Seasonal Trends

* Which seasons generate the highest sales revenue?
* How do customer purchasing patterns vary throughout the year?

### Customer Loyalty & Retention

* Do repeat customers generate more revenue?
* How does subscription status influence customer spending?

### Marketing & Promotion Analysis

* How do discounts impact purchase behavior?
* Are promo codes effective in driving sales?

### Operational Insights

* Which shipping methods are most preferred by customers?
* Which payment methods contribute the most revenue?

## KPI Calculations

The following business KPIs were calculated:

### Total Revenue

Total sales generated from all purchases.

### Total Customers

Total number of customers in the dataset.

### Average Purchase Value

Average amount spent per transaction.

### Average Review Rating

Average customer satisfaction rating.

### Total Subscribers

Number of customers with active subscriptions.

### Repeat Purchases

Total previous purchases made by customers.

## Key Business Insights

### Revenue Drivers

Clothing category contributes the highest share of overall revenue, indicating strong customer demand.

### Customer Behavior

Adult customers generate the majority of revenue compared to younger and senior age groups.

### Seasonal Performance

Sales remain relatively consistent across seasons with slight variations in revenue contribution.

### Customer Loyalty

Customers with higher previous purchase counts tend to generate greater revenue, highlighting the importance of retention strategies.

### Promotional Impact

Discounts and promo codes influence customer purchasing decisions and can be leveraged strategically to increase sales.

### Subscription Effectiveness

Subscribed customers demonstrate stronger engagement and contribute significantly to overall revenue.

## Business Recommendations

* Develop targeted marketing campaigns for high-value customer segments.
* Optimize promotional strategies using discounts and promo codes.
